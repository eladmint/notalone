## Chain Fusion Houses Model

Chain Fusion Houses appear to be physical/hybrid event spaces organized during major blockchain conferences. Key characteristics observed:

1. **Co-location Strategy**: Piggyback on major events (Token2049, Devcon, EthCC, KBW)
2. **Multi-event Format**: Multiple sub-events under one umbrella (9 events at Token2049)
3. **Diverse Organizer Ecosystem**: 5 types - Accelerators, VCs, Communities, Service Providers, Startups
4. **High Engagement**: 17,268 signups across 24 events
5. **Geographic Diversity**: Singapore, Bangkok, Brussels, Seoul
6. **Co-hosting Model**: Multiple organizers can co-host single events

## Analysis Notes

### Observations

1. **Community organizations dominate** - 11 of 24 organizers (46%)
2. **High-follower orgs don't always convert** - TaskOn has 753K followers but 0 signups
3. **Co-hosting is common** - VCs + Accelerators partner on same events
4. **Startups with product** - Omnity Network, Aethir bring large followings
5. **Service providers consistent** - All 3 delivered 800-1,100 signups
