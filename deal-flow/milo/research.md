## 1. Company Overview
[Confidence: HIGH]

### What They Do

Milo provides an AI-powered digital portfolio manager and crypto companion that allows users to:

- **Trade with natural language**: Execute trades by simply typing commands like "milo swap all my dust to USDC"
- **Automate portfolio management**: Deploy AI agents that trade autonomously based on user risk profiles and market conditions
- **Unify cross-chain operations**: Manage all chains and wallets through a single interface
- **Access simplified DeFi**: Navigate complex DeFi protocols with AI guidance

**Source:** Pitch deck pages 1, 4, 5, 7

### The Problem They Solve

**"Trading/Investing On Chain in 2025 is Still Broken"** (Deck page 3)

The market faces critical pain points validated by user research:

1. **Too many feeds, too many steps, too much risk**:
   - Multiple wallets per chain
   - Scattered trading tools
   - Complex DeFi interfaces
   - Disconnected data sources
   - Execution errors (wrong size, no stops, bad routes)

2. **User pain points** (Deck page 24):
   - 84% report missing opportunities
   - 76% struggle with portfolio management
   - 92% want simplified DeFi access
   - 68% fear making mistakes

3. **Management overhead**: Users stall, churn, or make expensive mistakes due to complexity

**How Milo solves this**:
Milo acts as a unified AI companion that simplifies the entire trading journey through four key pillars: Collaborate, Explain, Execute, and Optimize (Deck page 5).

**Source:** Pitch deck pages 3, 5, 24
