## Executive Summary

SHIFT is building the first MiCAR-compliant tokenized stocks platform, enabling 24/7 permissionless trading of real equities on decentralized exchanges (DEXs). The company offers Asset-Referenced Tokens (ARTs) backed 1:1 by underlying equities, with proof of reserves publicly available via Chainlink oracles. Currently raising $2M Seed at $20M FDV, backed by SNZ Holding (lead), PRIM3 VC, GMX founder, and dAppRadar founder. The platform targets the $4.76T DEX trading volume market with a compliant, institutional-grade solution that bridges traditional finance and DeFi.

## 1. Company Overview

### What They Do

SHIFT provides tokenized stocks and ETFs as Asset-Referenced Tokens (ARTs) that allow DeFi participants to:

- Trade real equities 24/7 on decentralized exchanges without management fees
- Access permissionless minting and redemption with 1:1 backing
- Utilize tokenized stocks across DeFi protocols for yield generation (LP vaults, lending)
- Maintain self-custody of regulated, compliant real-world asset tokens

### The Problem They Solve

The DeFi ecosystem and traditional capital markets exist in separate worlds:

1. **No DeFi Access to Equities**: The $18.9T+ DeFi market has no native access to stocks, bonds, or ETFs - participants cannot deploy capital into traditional asset classes
2. **Securities Classification Barrier**: Current Security Token Offerings (STOs) are classified as securities, limiting permissionless trading and DeFi integration
3. **Trading Hour Limitations**: Traditional markets operate limited hours (9:30am-4pm ET, M-F), while crypto markets run 24/7/365
4. **Custody Fragmentation**: Users must maintain separate accounts across TradFi brokerages and crypto exchanges

SHIFT solves this by issuing Asset-Referenced Tokens (ARTs) under EU MiCAR regulation - these are NOT classified as securities, enabling permissionless 24/7 trading on any DEX while maintaining full regulatory compliance and 1:1 backing.

## 5. Technology

### Core Technology Approach

SHIFT employs a hybrid on-chain/off-chain architecture to deliver regulated, fully-backed tokenized assets:

1. **On-Chain Layer**: Smart contracts (ERC-20/SPL Token-2022) handle token transfers, Chainlink oracles provide price feeds and proof of reserve verification
2. **Off-Chain Layer**: Traditional financial rails for custody (DekaBank), brokerage (Alpaca), and settlement
3. **Bridge Mechanism**: Atomic mint/burn operations synchronized with reserve deposits/withdrawals

### Technical Infrastructure

- **Smart Contract Audit**: ZOKYO Security - 100/100 score
- **KYC/AML Provider**: Sumsub
- **Financial Audit**: Ernst & Young Cyprus
- **Oracle Network**: Chainlink Build Program member
- **Multi-Chain**: Solana (primary), Base (L2), Etherlink (Tezos L2)

### Proof of Reserve Architecture

```
[User Request] --> [Smart Contract] --> [Chainlink Oracle]
                                              |
                                              v
[Reserve Custodian (DekaBank)] <-- [Verification Query]
                                              |
                                              v
                                    [On-Chain Attestation]
```

#### Synthetic Alternatives (GMX, Helix)
- **Focus**: Synthetic perpetuals with stock exposure
- **Differentiator**: No actual asset backing, purely synthetic/derivative exposure
- **Limitation**: Not true tokenized stocks, counterparty/protocol risk

### Competitive Advantages

1. **MiCAR-First Compliance**: Only fully MiCAR-compliant tokenized stocks platform (vs Swiss DLT Act for Backed, proprietary for Robinhood)
2. **True Permissionless**: Asset-Referenced Tokens (ARTs) enable fully permissionless secondary trading on any DEX - not possible with securities classification
3. **DeFi-Native Architecture**: Built for composability with DeFi protocols from day one (LP vaults, lending) vs centralized exchange focus
4. **Physical Redemption**: Option to redeem for underlying shares, not just cash settlement
5. **Proof of Reserve**: Real-time on-chain verification via Chainlink vs periodic attestations

### Competitive Landscape Summary

The tokenized securities market is rapidly consolidating with major players making significant moves in 2025. Kraken's acquisition of Backed ($10B volume), Robinhood's L2 launch (1,000+ stocks), and institutional interest from Citadel Securities signal massive market validation. SHIFT differentiates through MiCAR compliance (enabling true permissionless trading without securities classification) and DeFi-native architecture (vs CEX-first competitors). The key competitive battleground is distribution and liquidity - incumbents have significant advantages here, but SHIFT's regulatory positioning may enable unique DeFi integrations unavailable to securities-classified competitors.
