## 1. Company Overview
[Confidence: MEDIUM]

### What They Do

Tweem provides a prediction market platform built on social attention, allowing users to:

- **Predict tweet virality**: Browse curated tweets and bet on whether they'll become "bangers" (viral hits) or flop
- **Rapid feedback loops**: Unlike traditional prediction markets (elections, sports) that take weeks/months to resolve, tweet virality resolves within hours
- **Social skill monetization**: Convert understanding of internet culture and trend-spotting ability into financial returns

**Source:** [Tweem.lol website](https://www.tweem.lol/), [Crypto Mate article](https://cryptom8.com/11/01/tweem-lol/)

### The Problem They Solve

**Problem 1: Slow Resolution in Traditional Prediction Markets**
Traditional prediction markets (Polymarket, Kalshi) focus on events that take weeks, months, or years to resolve (elections, sports championships, macro events). This creates long capital lockup periods and delayed gratification.

**Problem 2: Untapped Social Media Prediction Category**
Despite Twitter/X being a primary cultural battleground where trends emerge and die within hours, there was no liquid prediction market specifically for social media virality.

**Problem 3: Barrier Between Social Activity and Financial Markets**
Users spend hours on social media developing intuition about what content resonates, but had no way to monetize this skill beyond traditional content creation.

**Tweem's Solution:**
Tweem creates a fast-paced prediction market specifically for tweet virality, enabling users to:
- Place bets on viral potential within minutes
- Get resolution and feedback within hours (not weeks)
- Monetize their social media intuition directly
- Participate in multiple prediction cycles per day

**Sources:** [Crypto Mate](https://cryptom8.com/11/01/tweem-lol/), [0Gaming](https://0gaming.com/tweem/)
